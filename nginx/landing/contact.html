Privacy and data protection (PDPO)

For Data Access Requests, Data Correction Requests, opt-outs and any question relating to the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486):
info@lovemuse.app (subject line: "Privacy request")

We respond to PDPO requests within 40 days, as required by the Ordinance.

Content reports and appeals

To report content that breaks our Community Guidelines or to appeal a moderation decision:
info@lovemuse.app (subject line: "Content report" or "Appeal")

Copyright (DMCA-style) notices

If you believe content on Muse infringes your copyright, send us a notice including (a) identification of the work, (b) the URL or screenshot of the allegedly infringing content, (c) your contact details, and (d) a good faith statement that you are the rights holder or an authorised agent:
info@lovemuse.app

Law-enforcement requests

Authorised requests from Hong Kong authorities (e.g. under the PDPO, the Crimes Ordinance or the Personal Data (Privacy) (Amendment) Ordinance 2021) should be sent to info@lovemuse.app. We require service on official letterhead identifying the authority, the legal basis, and a specific scope.

Regulatory authority

Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data, Hong Kong (PCPD)
12/F, Sunlight Tower, 248 Queen's Road East, Wanchai, Hong Kong
www.pcpd.org.hk
