Community Guidelines

Last updated: 18 May 2026

Muse is a place to discover and share restaurants with the people you trust. Honest, kind and useful contributions are what make the feed worth opening. These Guidelines tell you what we expect from everyone using the Service and what will get content or accounts removed.

1. Be honest

  • Only rate restaurants you have actually visited.
  • Don't accept payment or freebies in exchange for a positive rating.
  • Don't post fake reviews for a competitor's venue or for your own.

2. Be respectful

  • No harassment, threats, hate speech, or attacks on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age or any other protected characteristic.
  • Criticise the food, not the staff. Naming and shaming individual workers is not allowed.

3. Keep it legal

Do not post content that breaks the law, including Hong Kong laws on defamation, obscenity (Cap. 390), incitement, intellectual property infringement, or unsolicited commercial messages (Cap. 593). Doxxing — publishing private contact details of identifiable individuals — is prohibited and may also breach the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486).

4. Respect privacy

  • Don't post photos of identifiable people without their consent.
  • Don't share personal data (phone numbers, home addresses, ID numbers, financial information) of other people.
  • Treat friends' shared activity as private to that friendship: don't republish it elsewhere without permission.

5. No spam, no scams

  • No bulk promotion, affiliate spam, or repeated unsolicited messages.
  • No phishing, fake links, malware, or impersonation of Muse staff.

6. Adult content & safety

Pornographic or sexually explicit content, gratuitous violence, and content that sexualises minors are prohibited and will be reported to the relevant authorities. Content that promotes self-harm or suicide is not permitted; if you are in distress, please contact the Samaritans Hong Kong on 2896 0000.

7. Intellectual property

Only post photos and text you have the right to share. If you believe your copyright has been infringed, email info@lovemuse.app with the URL or screenshot, identification of the work and a statement that you are the rights holder.

8. Reporting and enforcement

If you see content that violates these Guidelines, report it by emailing info@lovemuse.app. We will review the report. Depending on severity and history, we may:

  • remove the content;
  • warn the user;
  • suspend or terminate the account;
  • report the matter to law enforcement when legally required.

9. Appeals

If you believe your content or account was actioned in error, reply to the notification email within 30 days. We will review the appeal and respond.
